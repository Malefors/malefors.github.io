How to open your AnswersForms in Excel


  1. Make a special folder for your exercises either in your documents or on your desktop. Download your AnswersForm and save it in the special folder you just made. Do not open the answers form yet.

  2. Open the folder where you saved the AnswersForm. Right click the File and choose properties. Check the Unblock Box. Press the OK button.

  3. Open a blank Work Bok in Excel.

4. Go to File/options

5. Click Trust Center

6. Click trust center Settings

    7. Click Enable all Macros and check Trust access to the VBA project object model
    Click the OK button

    8. Click the OK button in the excel Options window.

    9. Click File/Open/Browse

    10. Browse to your special folder and open your AnwersDoc

11. Click Enable Editing.
