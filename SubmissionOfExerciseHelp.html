Submission of exercise


Every course member must submit their individual answers to the exercise. Since the instructions contain individual data your answers must be the answers to the instruction with your name on it, not somebody else's instruction.


Download your personal answering form, your personal data document and save it in your personal file area (your documents or desktop). The answering form must contain the corresponding answers to the questions based on your personal data in the data document belonging to your Answers form. It should be obvious to which questions and exercises the answers belong. You can choose weather you want direct feedback on your answers as you put in the answers or to get the feedback at the end when you have completed the form. Either way you can go back and correct your wrong answers in the form. If you chose direct feedback there will be a text box comment for the multiple choice questions if the answer is wrong, on that the answer is wrong, and why it is wrong, which will stay for a little more than 11 sec and then you can close it. For both the numerical- and the multiple choice questions there will be a remaining frown face if the answer is wrong or a smiley if it is right. If you choose no feedback before the form is completed you will not get any feedback. You may change between direct feed back- and no feedback mode at any time when the form is open except when you are editing the content of a cell.


When you are done, turn on direct feedback and check that your answers are right. Do not forget to save your completed answering form before you submit it to Canvas.
