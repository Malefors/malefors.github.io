The Heat Capacity is the total heat capacity for the house, i. e. it is the energy needed raise the steady state temperature in the house 1K. The Heat capacity corresponds to

m•c

in the formula

ΔE = m•c•ΔT

where

ΔE = the energy needed to raise the temperature in the house from our reference temperature 0 K to the starting temperature for our simulation

m = Mass of the house

c = a weighed avarage of the heat capcities of the materials in the house


Hence we can use the product of the linked variables


[House Mass]*[House Specific Heat]


in the auxillary Heat Capacity.
