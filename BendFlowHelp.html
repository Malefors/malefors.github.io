How to create a bent flow arrow

1. Click the flow tool in the top pane of StockSD with the left mouse button once.









2. Place the cursor where you want the beginning of the arrow and start drawing by holding down the left mouse button. Do not release the left button untill you have completed the arrow.








3. while holding down the left mouse button and drawing, click the right mouse button once whenever you want a bend on the arrow.



How to move the auxillary symbol on the bent flow arrow around











4. Release the left mouse button when the arrow is completed.
