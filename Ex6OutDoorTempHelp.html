In order to generate a mean yearly temperature curve, we use the expression: 7+12*Sin(2*Pi*T()/(365*24)-Pi/2) . (12 is to get an amplitude of 24, (365*24) is the number of hours during a year, 7 is to move the minimum of the curve from -12 to -5 and -Pi/2 is to get the curve starting at minimum temperature, at the firsth of january.
