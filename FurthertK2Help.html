The value of K2 is one of two subjects for our parameter estimation were we use the ”Tools\Optim” algoritm included in SochSD to minimise the Object function V wich is a measure of how close the behaviour of the model is to the real system. (In this case how the indoor temperature behaves in relation to heating and out door temperature.)

K2 corresponds to ΣU•A + Qv•C•δ in model I.

where

Qv = Volume metric flow

C = Specific heat

δ = Density


for the incoming ventilation air.
