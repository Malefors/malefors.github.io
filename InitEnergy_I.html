To calculate the initial energy for our simulation we use the physical law:

ΔE = m•c•ΔT


where

ΔE = the energy needed to raise the temperature in the house from our reference temperature 0 K to the starting temperature for our simulation

m = Mass of the house

c = a weighed avarage of the heat capcities of the materials in the house

and

ΔT = the difference between the initial temperature and our reference temperature 0 K


But in the model we have created an auxillary


Heat Capacity = m•c


Hence, we can set the initial value for the stock Energy as the product of the linked variables:


[Heat Capacity]*[Initial Temperature]
