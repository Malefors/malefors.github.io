Here we want the heating in the model to behave as the real heating in the house. Thus it is to be turned off for the first 12 hours and then turned on for the remaining time of the experiment.

Use the Step function under Mathematical functions

Thus use : Step(12, x), where you replace x with the value in your instructions, the Table 2 column ”Heating”, when the heating has been turned on again after being turned off, (= 0), for 12 hours.
