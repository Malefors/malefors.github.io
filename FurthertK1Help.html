The value of K1 is one of two subjects for our parameter estimation were we use the ”Tools\Optim” algoritm included in SochSD to minimise the Object function V wich is a measure of how close the behaviour of the model is to the real system. (In this case how the indoor temperature behaves in relation to heating and out door temperature.)

K1 is the total heat capacity for the house, i. e. it is the energy needed raise the steady state temperature in the house 1°C. K1 corresponds to

m•c

in the formula

ΔE = m•c•ΔT

where

ΔE = the energy needed to raise the temperature in the house from our reference temperature 0ºC to the starting temperature for our simulation

m = Mass of the house

c = a weighed avarage of the heat capcities of the materials in the house
