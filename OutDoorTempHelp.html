You find your data for the out door temperature in Tabel 2 in your personal instructions. The column Time corresponds to x1, x2.xn . The column Out Door Temp corresponds to the y1, y2… yn. Take the time to enter the data carefully into the StockSD converter exactly according to the requested syntax manually. The alternative is to copy the data from the instructions pdf file into Excel and re- arrange the data in excel according to the requested syntax and then paste the data into the StockSD converer.

Note that the last xn and yn are not supposed to be followed by a semicolon ; .
